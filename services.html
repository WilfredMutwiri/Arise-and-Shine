Climate change

We believe in nature and the entire ecosystem as a component to spur sustainable development. As in out vision, we endeavor to ensure not only the members but also the community at large has the required knowledge on sustainable use of the natural resources where's conserving the environment to support economic growth.
This is done through encouraging planting and growing of more trees, eradicating farming methods that lead to deforestation, water management and encouraging proper farming techniques. Aware of the negative effects of climate change, we also carry our sensitization to help to society develop the Resilience against any shocks and stresses that come with it while developing holistic community approach towards reducing the risks and disasters that come with climate change
