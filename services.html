Self-Empowerment

Self empowerment: At wake and shine we believe in everyone becoming the best of him/herself. we have a strong belief that individual belief translates into community development. We ensure that all our team members embark on a journey within themselves. Discovering their true self, passions, and purpose for a life filled with meaning.
We dive into embracing the newborns of our team members and visiting the couples within us, as we believe in the power of a family. In addition, we support individual career growth by providing the relevant opportunities to our members according to their different professionalisms. in this endeavor, we ensure all our members grow in their own aspects in life. together we grow as a family!

Community Empowerment

Welcome to a community where empowerment is not just a word; it's a collective force that propels us to new heights. At Wake and Shine, we believe in the extraordinary impact of united strength. We believe and support collective community development for a conducive environment for individual and group growth.

Join us as we undertake various development activities within our community that transcends boundaries and transforms lives through our tournaments and Youth empowerment seminars, environmental conservations, awareness meetings on various developments and topics, advocacy and addressing various cross cutting issues.

Finance And Investment

Welcome to Wake and Shine finance hub, where we demystify finance and unlock the doors to wealth creation. Dive into a world of smart decisions, strategic investments, and financial empowerment. We embrace economically viable investment as a driver towards sustainable development.

To support this, we do not only ensure that we have proper management of our finances and resources by ensuring that the handlers of our finances are competent enough, but by also ensuring that them, the members, and our fellow community youths receive financial literacy training. Our aim is to ensure a prudent and efficient finance management by the members and of the group in general while at the same time putting in place strategic investments in areas of interest.

Climate change

We believe in nature and the entire ecosystem as a component to spur sustainable development. As in out vision, we endeavor to ensure not only the members but also the community at large has the required knowledge on sustainable use of the natural resources where's conserving the environment to support economic growth.

This is done through encouraging planting and growing of more trees, eradicating farming methods that lead to deforestation, water management and encouraging proper farming techniques. Aware of the negative effects of climate change, we also carry our sensitization to help to society develop the Resilience against any form of shocks and stresses that may arise from climate change and in specific drought.
