~~Wake and Shine

Wake and Shine Self Help group is an established social group registered under the social services department, registration number; THA/DSS/SH/4163.
Since it's establishement, the group has been of great help to the residents of Tharaka South district in Tharaka-Nithi county. The group is focused on ensuring community development and Advancementof the youth through support and mentorship.

~ Our Vision~

An empowered community with the understanding of utilizing resources rationally for living dignified lives.

~ Our Mission ~

We seek to increase the capacity of the members and the community to live dignified lives through promotion of; members welfare, access to education, environmental conservation, youth/marginalized empowerment, diversification of livelihoods and human rights stewardship.

~ Core Values~

  • Integrity and Accountability
  • Professionalism
  • Partnership
  • Stewardship
  • Teamwork
  • Human dignity
