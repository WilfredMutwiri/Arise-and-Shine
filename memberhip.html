Our Membership!

Step 1
Make a decision to join us

Step 2
Visit our website and check our requirements

Step 3
Contact one of our leaders for a registration form

Step 4
Pay for the registration form

Step 5
Collect the registration form and fill

Step 6
Return the registration form to the secretariat

Step 7
Attend a membership recruitment interview after receiving a communication

Step 8
Await interview results

Step 9
Receive communication regarding status of your application.

Step 10
Confirm acceptance of the results of the interview and pay the registration fee. You are now a member of Wake and shine. Congratulations and welcome.
